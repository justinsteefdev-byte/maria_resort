About Us

Experience Nature's Embrace

Located in the serene hills of Munnar, Skyline Homestay offers a perfect blend of comfort and nature. Wake up to the misty mountains and the calming sight of the lake right from your window.

Whether you are a family looking for a fun getaway, a couple seeking a romantic retreat, or a group of friends wanting to explore, our doors are open. We pride ourselves on our warm hospitality, delicious home-cooked meals, and a staff that treats you like family.

  • Stunning Mountain & Lake Views
  • Home-Cooked Kerala Cuisine
